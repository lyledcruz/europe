      Prague Guide: Sights, Eats, Pubs, Restaurants & Local Tips

      Getting In & Around

      • Airport ‚Üí City Centre: Take Bus 119 (now often replaced with Trolleybus 59), connect to Green Metro; ~35 min, ticket: 40 CZK.
      • Public Transport: Reliable, fast, cheap. Buy 1-day or 3-day ticket at airport/train station, validate before first ride. Single ticket: 40 CZK. Inspectors active.
      • Avoid: Taxis (especially at train stations‚Äîoverpriced), Hop-On-Hop-Off buses, Airport Express bus.
      • City is walkable, trams and metro reach all areas. Ferries & buses available.

      Money & Payments

      • Currency: Czech crowns (CZK), not Euros/Dollars.
      • Best Tip: Pay by card/phone ("tap" culture). Rarely need cash.
      • If exchanging cash: Avoid airport & most central exchange offices, rates are bad even if ‚Äú0% fee.‚Äù
      • If using ATM: Always decline conversion to avoid extra fees. Prefer big-bank ATMs (ƒåSOB, KB, Raiffeisen).
      • Never exchange money on the street.

      • Moravian Wine, Czech Kofola (local cola). Absinthe in shops: touristy, not real, overpriced.
